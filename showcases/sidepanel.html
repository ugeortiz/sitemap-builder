Side Panel

Source: Figma — Lexicon Components. Right-anchored drawer. Default 280px wide (--lg = 480). Header (title + close), scrollable body and a top-bordered footer of action buttons.

Default 280px · large 480px

Side Panel Title

The side panel is a vertical drawer pinned to the right edge of the viewport. It's wider than a popover and used for editing forms or detail views without leaving the page.


Side Panel Large

The large variant accommodates more content — for instance a 2-column form sheet or a detailed entity editor.
